    Greek Context

    Delta: Proxy for probability of expiring ITM. For covered calls, 0.30–0.40 balances income and retention.

    Gamma: Measures how quickly Delta changes. High Gamma means assignment risk can rise sharply late in the cycle.

    Theta: Time decay. This is the income engine of covered calls.
